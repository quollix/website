---
title: "Quollix 1.2.0"
date: 2026-06-28
---

Quollix 1.2.0 is a small feature release focused on connecting servers and improving account administration.

The main addition is [federation]({{< relref "docs/usage/federation/_index.md" >}}). Quollix can now act as an OIDC provider or OIDC client, which makes it possible to connect multiple Quollix servers or use an external identity provider for sign-in.

Administrators can also [disable users]({{< relref "docs/usage/users.md#disabling-users" >}}) without deleting their accounts. Disabled users cannot sign in, existing sessions are removed, and the account can be enabled again later.
