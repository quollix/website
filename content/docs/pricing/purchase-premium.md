title: "Purchase Premium"
---

This page explains how to purchase a Quollix Premium subscription and activate premium features with a license key.

## Purchase a License

1. Open [Purchase Quollix Premium](https://quollix.lemonsqueezy.com/checkout/buy/f4387d1a-157b-49b7-8d45-19c8ce345dbc?media=0&discount=0).
2. Complete the checkout through Lemon Squeezy.
3. After purchase, Lemon Squeezy shows the license key on the confirmation page. Open the key details to copy it.
4. Lemon Squeezy also sends the license key by email together with the purchase receipt.

The license key is a 36-character code and may look like this:

```text
00000000-0000-0000-0000-000000000000
```

## Activate Premium in Quollix

1. Open the **Premium** page in your Quollix instance.
2. Enter the license key and save it.
3. After activation, premium features become available in the navigation sidebar.

## Subscription Management

Subscriptions are billed monthly and can be cancelled through Lemon Squeezy. After cancellation, premium features remain available until the end of the current billing period.
