Quollix is available in community and premium editions. A complete feature comparison by edition is available on the [Features]({{< ref "docs/introduction/features.md" >}}) page. Licensing details are summarized on the [Licensing]({{< relref "../about/licensing.md" >}}) page.

To purchase a subscription and activate premium features, see [Purchase Premium]({{< relref "purchase-premium.md" >}}).

## Editions

* **Community Edition**
  * Price: Free
  * No subscription required
* **Premium Edition**
  * Price: 15 € per month
  * Billed monthly
  * VAT or applicable sales tax is calculated at checkout by Lemon Squeezy, the Merchant of Record.

## Billing and Cancellation

- Subscriptions are billed on a monthly basis
- You can cancel your subscription at any time
- After cancellation, access to premium features remains available until the end of the current billing period
