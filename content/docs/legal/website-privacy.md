_Last Updated: 26.02.2026_

This Privacy Policy applies to visits to the publicly accessible Quollix website.

## 1. Controller

The controller within the meaning of applicable data protection laws is the person identified in the Imprint.

## 2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies when you visit the Website for informational purposes.

## 3. Server Log Data

When you access the Website, technical data is automatically processed by the hosting infrastructure. This may include:

- IP address
- Date and time of access
- Requested pages or resources
- Referrer information
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Technical request metadata

This data is processed to ensure security, stability, and proper operation of the Website.

The legal basis for this processing is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in operating a secure and stable website).

## 4. Cookies

The Website does not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes.

## 5. Hosting

The Website is hosted by a service provider located in Germany. Personal data may be processed on servers operated by the hosting provider for the purpose of delivering the Website.

## 6. Data Retention

Server log data is retained only for as long as necessary for security, troubleshooting, and abuse prevention, and is regularly deleted or rotated.

## 7. Data Sharing

Personal data is not sold and is not shared with third parties except where necessary for technical operation of the Website.

## 8. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law, you may have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, and objection.

Requests may be submitted using the contact details provided in the Imprint.

## 9. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect legal or operational changes.
